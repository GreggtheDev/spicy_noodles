Step-by-step Directions To Make Spicy Sriracha Noodles

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the sauce for the noodles. In a small bowl stir together the brown sugar, soy sauce and sriracha.
    Set the sauce aside.
  2. Bring a pot of water to a boil. once boiling, add the noodles and boil until tender.
    Drain the noodles in a colander.
  3. Melt the butter over medium heat. In a large skillet or wok, melt the butter, then add red pepper.
    Stir briefly.
  4. Add the eggs.Crack the eggs into the skillet and scramble them with the melted butter and crushed red pepper.
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the skillet and mix them with the scrambled eggs.
  6. Pour the prepared sauce over the noodles and continue mixing until everything is well-combined and heated through.
  7. Remove from heat and garnish with fresh cilantro and sliced green onions.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
